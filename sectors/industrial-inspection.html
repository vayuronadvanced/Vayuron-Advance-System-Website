Overview

INDUSTRY
CONTEXT

Industrial operators — oil refineries, chemical plants, power stations, and manufacturing facilities — face constant pressure to reduce inspection costs, improve safety, and maximize uptime.

Vayuron's industrial inspection platform enables automated aerial inspection of live and shutdown plants, with AI defect detection delivering consistent, actionable reports directly to maintenance management systems.

The Problem

KEY
CHALLENGES

Confined Space Access

Tanks, ducts, and confined industrial spaces are dangerous and slow to inspect manually.

Downtime Costs

Shutting down production for manual inspection is expensive; aerial methods enable live-plant inspection.

Inconsistent Documentation

Manual inspection quality varies by inspector; automated systems provide consistent, comparable reports.

Predictive Maintenance Gaps

Without continuous inspection data, maintenance is reactive rather than predictive, increasing failure risk.

Our Approach

VAYURON'S
SOLUTION

Vayuron deploys its four core technology domains — UAV Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Software Platforms, and Advanced Engineering — in a coordinated stack tailored specifically for industrial inspection environments.

The result is an end-to-end indigenous capability: data collection through autonomous platforms, intelligent processing through onboard and cloud AI, actionable visibility through secure dashboards, and ruggedized hardware engineered for the specific conditions of this sector.
