TARGETED SPRAYING PROGRAMME

Spray UAV deployment across 800 hectares of paddy fields, demonstrating measurable reduction in input costs and chemical use.

The Work

WHAT WAS
DEPLOYED

4 spray UAVs operated

4 spray UAVs operated in coordinated relay shifts over 5 field days

GPS-guided spray routes

GPS-guided spray routes generated from prior multispectral survey data

Variable-rate spraying

Variable-rate spraying adjusted chemical dosage based on field zone severity

Post-spray NDVI survey

Post-spray NDVI survey confirmed uniform application coverage across target zones

Results

MEASURABLE
OUTCOMES

Chemical use reduced by 42% vs blanket spraying baseline
Labour requirement reduced from 80 person-days to 12 operator-days
Input cost saving of ₹3.2 Lakh across the 800 ha area
