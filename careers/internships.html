How It Works

PROGRAMME
DETAILS

Duration

3 to 6 months, with possible extension for outstanding interns. Applications are open throughout the year.

Disciplines

Aerospace Engineering, Computer Vision & AI, Full-Stack Software, Composite Manufacturing, and Flight Operations.

Live Projects

Interns are assigned to active projects from day one. No dummy assignments — your work ships into real systems.

Stipend

Competitive monthly stipend commensurate with discipline and project complexity. Details shared at offer stage.

Mentorship

Each intern is assigned a senior engineer mentor who guides technical development and career growth throughout the programme.

PPO Opportunity

Outstanding interns receive Pre-Placement Offers for full-time roles before the end of their programme.
