WHAT WE
OFFER

Mission-Driven Work

We prioritize reliability, safety, and mission success above all else. Your code flies drones, your algorithms defend borders, your designs get deployed — not just demoed.

Fast Career Growth

Every team member is expected to act as a builder and problem solver. You will lead systems, not just contribute to them — rapid progression for engineers who deliver.

Learn Across Domains

We embrace learning, experimentation, and improvement — working at the intersection of aerospace, AI, and software, with teams that cross-train across disciplines.

Indigenous Focus

We build everything in India, for India. Every engineer here contributes to reducing national dependency on foreign defence technology.

Competitive Package

Market-rate salaries, performance bonuses, health coverage, and flexible work arrangements for the right candidates.

Field Exposure

Engineers at Vayuron see their systems deployed. Field visits, flight tests, and client demonstrations are part of the role.
