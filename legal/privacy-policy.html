PRIVACY
POLICY

How Vayuron Advanced Systems collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

1. Who We Are

Vayuron Advanced Systems Pvt. Ltd. ("Vayuron", "we", "us", or "our") is an Indian private limited company engaged in the design and development of autonomous systems, artificial intelligence, and advanced engineering solutions. Our registered office is located at Advanced Technology Centre, India.

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data collected through our website at vayuron.in and through any direct communications with our team.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and organisation — submitted via our contact or inquiry form
  • Nature of inquiry and message content — submitted via our contact form
  • Email address — submitted through newsletter subscriptions, contact forms, career applications, or other communication channels on our website
  • Browser type, IP address, and usage data — collected automatically via server logs
  • Cookies and session data — see our Cookie Policy

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiry or demo request submitted through our contact form
  • To send you newsletters or updates you have subscribed to (unsubscribe available at any time)
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors engage with our content
  • To comply with legal obligations applicable to our business

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers located in India. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmissions on this website use TLS encryption.

Where we engage third-party service providers (for example, email service providers or cloud hosting), we ensure they adhere to equivalent data protection standards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected. Contact form submissions are retained for 24 months. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. Server log data is retained for 90 days.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law and international best practice, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant regulatory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@vayuronadvancedsystems.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
