SECURITY
POLICY

Our approach to website and data security, and how to responsibly report a vulnerability.

Last Updated: June 2025

Our Security Commitment

Vayuron Advanced Systems takes the security of our website, systems, and customer data seriously. Given the nature of our work in defence and critical infrastructure technology, we apply rigorous security standards across all digital systems we develop and operate.

Website Security Measures

  • All pages served over HTTPS with TLS 1.3 encryption
  • HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) enforced
  • Contact form data transmitted with end-to-end encryption
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers to prevent XSS attacks
  • Regular security reviews and dependency audits
  • No storage of sensitive user data beyond what is described in our Privacy Policy

Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in our website or digital systems, we ask you to report it to us responsibly before making it public.

Report vulnerabilities to:
Email: info@vayuronadvancedsystems.com
Subject line: SECURITY DISCLOSURE — [Provide Brief Description to us]

What to Include in Your Report

  • A description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots (if applicable)
  • Your contact information for follow-up

Our Commitments to Researchers

  • We will acknowledge your report within 5 business days
  • We will keep you informed of the status of the fix
  • We will not take legal action against researchers who follow this responsible disclosure process
  • We will credit researchers who identify valid vulnerabilities (with their consent)

Out of Scope

The following are outside the scope of our security disclosure programme:

  • Social engineering of Vayuron personnel
  • Denial of service attacks against our infrastructure
  • Physical security testing
  • Vulnerabilities in third-party services we use (report directly to those providers)

Note on Defence Systems

Security vulnerabilities in Vayuron's field-deployed defence and industrial systems are outside the scope of this website security disclosure programme and must be reported through appropriate government and regulatory channels.
